Tell the Department for Communities how your disability affects you for your PIP claim

Only use this service if you have already started your application for Personal Independence Payment (PIP) by phone and have received an email from the Department for Communities (DfC).

Use the service to:

  • tell DfC how your health condition or disability affects you
  • upload any supporting documentary evidence of your condition or disability and how it affects you

You will be asked questions about your ability to carry out everyday activities. The answers will help DfC understand the effect of your health condition or disability on your everyday life and assess your entitlement to PIP.

Take your time and describe all the help you need. You do not have to answer all the questions in one go. You can save your progress at any point and continue later.

Before you start

You will need:

  • the email address used by DfC to contact you
  • your National Insurance number
  • your mobile phone
