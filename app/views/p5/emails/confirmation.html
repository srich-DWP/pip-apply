We will send your information to a health professional to consider, before we make our decision. If we need more information about how your health condition or disability affects your daily life we may invite you to a telephone assessment.

If you have uploaded any supporting evidence

We will include this in your claim to PIP and will consider this before we make our decision.

If you need to upload more supporting evidence

If you need to upload any more evidence that might support your application you will need to sign in again. Go to: [URL of sign in page]

You might find it useful to bookmark the sign in page in your internet browser or keep this email.

If anything changes or you need help

Please call us if any of the details you gave us have changed or if you need help with your claim.

If you do not want to continue with your PIP claim, please let us know.

You can call us on 0800 121 4433 or Textphone: 0800 121 4493 if you have speech or hearing difficulties.
