Register

Register so you can securely sign in whenever you use the Apply for PIP service.

Before you start

You will need:

  • the email address used by the Department for Work and Pensions to contact you
  • your National Insurance number
  • your mobile phone

What happens next

We will send a 6-digit security code to your mobile phone or email address to check that it belongs to you.

You will also need to create a password.

Whenever you sign in we will send a new security code to your mobile phone or email address.

We will check your details against our records to make sure we are dealing with the right person. This also helps protect you from fraud.
