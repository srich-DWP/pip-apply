Extra information – mixing with other people

Tell us more about any difficulties you have when mixing with other people:

  • tell us about how your condition affects you doing this activity
  • tell us how you manage at the moment and the problems you have when you can’t do this activity
  • do you have behaviours that could put yourself or others at risk?
  • does whether you can do this vary throughout the day? Tell us about good and bad days
  • do you avoid mixing with other people, some more than others?
  • does it take you a long time to mix with other people?
  • do you experience any other difficulties, either during or after the activity, like anxiety or distress?
  • tell us about help you need from another person when mixing with other people. This includes help you have and help you need but don’t get
