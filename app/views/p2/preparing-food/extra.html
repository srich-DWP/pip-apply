Extra information – preparing food

Tell us more about any difficulties you have when preparing and cooking food:

  • tell us how your condition affects you doing this activity
  • tell us how you manage at the moment and the problems you have when you cannot do this activity
  • tell us how long it takes to prepare and cook food
  • does whether you can do this vary throughout the day? Tell us about good and bad days
  • can you cook using an oven safely? If not, tell us why not
  • tell us about the aids or appliances you need to use to help you prepare and cook food
  • do you experience any other difficulties, either during or after the activity, like pain, breathlessness or tiredness?
  • tell us about the help you need from another person when preparing food. This includes help you have and help you need but do not get
