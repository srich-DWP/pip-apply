Upload supporting evidence for your PIP application

It is important that you help us to understand your needs by uploading any documentary evidence or information. This should explain how your health condition or disability affects your daily life.

Examples of documents you could upload include:

  • reports about you from health professionals
  • care or treatment plans
  • test results
  • repeat prescription lists

Help with the types of supporting evidence you should upload and send (PDF)

If you have any paper documents to upload, take photos or scans of them and save these to the device you are using.

Only upload copies of evidence or information you already have. If we need more, we will ask your health professional for it.

Any delay in sending evidence you have might mean:

  • your claim to PIP will take longer to assess
  • you might need an assessment with a health professional which might not have been necessary
  • we might not be able to get all the information we need to make a decision on your claim which accurately reflects your daily living or mobility needs

Try to avoid:

  • send general information about your condition like fact sheets or information from the internet
  • contact your GP or health professional to ask for new or additional information as they may charge you for this
