Apply for a UK Shared Prosperity Fund grant

If you're an organisation looking to support people facing multiple or complex needs across the UK, you can apply for a grant from the UK Shared Prosperity Fund.

How the fund works

Are you applying for the maximum in your invitation?

The funding is split between different geographical locations with invitations for each area. You can apply for funding for your area. The invitation details the criteria that has been set for each area.

How to apply

You can apply for a grant from the UK Shared Prosperity Fund by:

  • applying online yourself
  • applying through another organisation by partnering with them

What is an invitation?

Each area of the UK has an invitation that states what the local needs are. The invitations have been written through discussions with local authorities. These discussions have highlighted what local needs should be addressed in that invitation. Organisations can choose to apply for the invitation linked to their local area.

What you'll get

You'll get money to support a project that supports the needs of your local area. How much money you can apply for depends on the amount outlined in the invitation for your local area.
