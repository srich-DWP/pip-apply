  • 4a. Do you need to use an aid or appliance to eat and drink?
  • 4b. Do you use a feeding tube or similar device to eat or drink?

  • 4d. Extra information - Eating and drinking

5. Managing treatments

  • 5a. Do you need to use an aid or appliance to monitor your health conditions, take medication or manage home treatments?
  • 5b. Do you need help from another person to monitor your health conditions, take medication or manage home treatments?
  • 5c. Extra information - Managing treatments

6. Washing and bathing

  • 6a. Do you need to use an aid or appliance to wash and bathe yourself, including using a bath or shower?
  • 6b. Do you need help from another person to wash and bathe?
  • 6c. Extra information - Washing and bathing

7. Managing toilet needs

  • 7a. Do you need to use an aid or appliance to use the toilet or manage incontinence?
  • 7b. Do you need help from another person to use the toilet or manage incontinence?
  • 7c. Extra information - Managing toilet needs

8. Dressing and undressing

  • 8a. Do you need to use an aid or appliance to dress or undress?
  • 8b. Do you need help from another person to dress or undress?
  • 8c. Extra Information - Dressing and undressing

9. Communicating

  • 9a. Do you need to use an aid or appliance to communicate with others?
  • 9b. Do you need help from another person to communicate with others?
  • 9c. Extra information - Communicating

10. Reading

  • 10a. Do you need to use an aid or appliance other than spectacles or contact lenses to read signs, symbols and words?
  • 10b. Do you need help from another person to read or understand signs, symbols and words?
  • 10c. Extra information - Reading

11. Mixing with other people

  • 11a. Do you need another person to help you to mix with other people?
  • 11b. Do you find it difficult to mix with other people because of severe anxiety or distress?
  • 11c. Extra information - Mixing with other people

12. Making decisions about money

  • 12a. Do you need someone else to help you to understand how much things cost when you buy them or how much change you will receive?
  • 12b. Do you need someone else to help you manage your household budgets, pay bills or plan future purchases?
  • 12c. Extra information - Making decisions about money

13. Going out

  • 13a. Do you need help from another person to plan a route to somewhere you know well?
  • 13b. Do you need help getting to somewhere you do not know well?
  • 13c. Are you unable to go out because of severe anxiety or distress?
  • 13d. Extra information - Going out

14. Moving around

  • 14a. How far can you walk taking into account any aids you use?
  • 14b. Do you use an aid or appliance to walk?
  • 14c. Do you use a wheelchair or similar device to move around safely, reliably and repeatedly and in a reasonable time period?
  • 14d. Extra information - Moving around
